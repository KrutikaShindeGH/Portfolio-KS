    Published Article

    Publication

    Mario Ai Model Using Gaming Reinforcement Learning
    Journal of Instrumentation Technology & Innovations (JOITI)

    This study presents a comprehensive framework for training an intelligent agent to play the classic video game, Mario, through the application of reinforcement learning techniques. The proposed model combines the Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm with the potential integration of the Deep Q-Learning (DQL) algorithm to achieve enhanced gaming performance. The system setup involves configuring Mario within a gaming environment, running the game, and preprocessing the game state. Leveraging grayscale and vectorization techniques, the agent gains a more efficient representation of the game environment. We draw inspiration from key research papers such as ”System Design for an Integrated Lifelong Reinforcement Learning Agent for Real-Time Strategy Games” and ”A Survey of Deep Reinforcement Learning in Video Games” to refine our approach.
